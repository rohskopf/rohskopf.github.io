Fast and Accurate Interatomic Potentials.

Problem: All objects are made of atoms - us, the technology we use every day, all forms of nature, etc. The behavior of atoms therefore influences all of these important things, and we could predict or design the behavior of matter if we could model the dynamics of its underlying constituents - the atoms. One of my main research thrusts is therefore centered around accurately modelling atomic motions using molecular dynamics (MD) simulations.

Solution: The cause of atomic motions, however, is due to the interatomic potential - the interaction energy that atoms exert on each other. I model the interatomic potential by using machine learning to reproduce accurate quantum mechanical calculations.
